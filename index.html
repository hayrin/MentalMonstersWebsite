Why is mental health so important?

 

Did you know that data from a study based on 12.8% of 5-19 year olds, 
figures show cases of diagnose mental health issues rose from 1999 to 2017, when the study was carried out.

1 in 8 children aged 5-19 have a diagnosed mental health problem, with 5.5% also experiencing mental health disorders.



Knowing about mental health , it's affects and what can be done to help, are all things we can learn.
